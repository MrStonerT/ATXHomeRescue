Standards & compliance

What we do. What we don't.

Setting expectations upfront protects you, our pros, and our craft. Here's what's in scope at ATX Home Rescue, and what we'll honestly hand off to a specialist.

✓
Cabinet install & mounting

Wall cabinets, base cabinets, hardware, alignment. Studs hit, doors level.

In scope
✓
Flooring

Floating laminate, LVP, underlayment & moisture barrier, transitions, quarter-round.

In scope
✓
General handyman & smart-home

TV mounts, cameras, doorbells, thermostats, shelves, gallery walls, appliance installs.

In scope
✓
Drywall patch, texture & paint

Holes, dings, hairline cracks. Mesh + compound, orange-peel/knockdown texture match, primer and color-matched paint.

In scope
✕
Electrical work behind the wall

Plug-and-play smart devices, sure. New circuits, panels, rewires — we'll refer a licensed electrician.

Out of scope
✕
Plumbing past the shutoff

Faucet & fixture swaps, fine. Pipe replacement, slab leaks, water heaters — licensed plumber territory.

Out of scope
✕
Roofs, gas, & HVAC

Specialist trades. We won't quote them and we won't fake it — we'll point you to someone who's licensed.
