Good questions

Answers, upfront.

How does the flat price work?

Every bundle has a fixed scope and a fixed price — materials included unless noted. If Ranger can't match your project to a bundle, we'll quote it from photos and text, and that number is the number. No hourly creep.

Who actually does the work?

Insured, vetted contractors. Right now we run one crew of two full-time pros based here in NW Austin. As we grow, we'll keep the bar the same.

What areas do you cover?

Northwest Austin, specifically: 78726, 78727, 78729, 78730, 78731, 78750, 78759. Neighborhoods include Anderson Mill, Jester Estates, Great Hills, Balcones Woods, Spicewood, and around.

How fast can you get out?

Most bookings happen within the same week. Urgent? Tell Ranger — we'll see what's open today or tomorrow.

Do you do big remodels?

Not yet. We're focused on small-to-medium projects: handyman work, seasonal maintenance, trim & carpentry, deck & fence repair, appliance installs. If it's a bathroom gut-job, we'll tell you honestly.

How do I pay?

Through Jobber Payments — credit card, debit, ACH. You pay a small deposit at booking; the balance is billed when the job is done.
